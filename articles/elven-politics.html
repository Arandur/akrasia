Elven Politics

The elven race comprises one unified nation, governed by a council of eight wizards known officially as the First Council after the Order of Denali, colloquially as the First Council or the High Council. Each council seat is associated with one of the eight schools of magic.

The members of the First Council are held to be the greaters teachers of wizardry in elvenkind – not necessarily the greatest or most powerful wizards themselves. Council members hold their seats for life or until ousted by a successful challenge.

History

The First Council was established and received its mandate and authority from Denali at the Dawn of the First Age, at the Genesis Moot, three hundred years ago.
